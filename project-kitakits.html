Project Case Study

KitaKits - Financial Literacy App

A simulation-first financial literacy platform designed to help Filipino students practice real-world financial decisions through interactive scenarios before managing actual money.

Note: This repository contains the design, research, and product planning for KitaKits. This project was developed as part of a technopreneurship requirement and is being considered for further development in the future.

Project overview

What it is, who it's for, and the outcome.

  • Context: Technopreneurship project focused on financial education innovation
  • Role: Product design lead, market researcher, business model strategist
  • Timeline: Team project (4 members)
  • Impact: Created comprehensive financial literacy platform concept with market validation and business sustainability model

Technologies used

Design and research tools.

Figma Google Forms Google Docs Google Sheets Canva

Problem

The challenge we addressed in financial education.

Financial literacy education is mostly theoretical. Students are expected to manage budgeting, taxes, and investments without ever practicing real financial decisions, leading to confusion and fear of making costly mistakes. This creates a significant barrier to financial confidence and real-world money management skills.

Solution

Our approach and the platform we designed to solve financial literacy challenges.

  • Designed a simulation-based platform for risk-free financial decision practice
  • Created interactive timeline visualization for long-term outcome understanding
  • Developed strategy comparison tools for learning from different financial approaches
  • Built sustainable freemium business model for accessibility and scalability

Key Features

Core platform functionality and capabilities.

  • Customizable financial simulations (budgeting, investing, taxes)
  • Timeline slider for long-term outcome visualization
  • Strategy comparison across financial decisions
  • Personal financial portfolios and risk profile badges
  • Institutional dashboard for classroom integration
  • Freemium model with premium feature unlocks

Challenges

Obstacles we encountered and how we overcame them.

  • Realistic simulation design: Balanced complexity with beginner-friendly interface
  • Freemium model optimization: Structured features to maximize user value
  • Complex concept simplification: Translated financial concepts into intuitive UI/UX
  • Market validation: Aligned product features with real user needs through research
